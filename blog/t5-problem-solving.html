Problem Solving

Jan 30 2018 by Frank

Tell your non-tech friend a story about a time you:

1. Got blocked on a simple problem.
2. Solved a problem in an elegant way In both cases:

What was the problem?
What problem solving techniques did you use?
How did you feel throughout the process?
What did you learn?

I met so many problem during work and study. I just cannot address one. Usually when I have a problem, I always google first and it can solve 90% of them. Otherwise I will ask my colleagues. It's also a quick way to get the answer. We used to have short audio conference on every Monday. We update the current progress, blocking issues and new ideas. It's a good chance to unblock your problems by listen to others ideas.
